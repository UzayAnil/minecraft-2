How To Survive

Start by punching trees down as you spawn in a new world. Collect more than 5 logs and convert 3 into planks. Then, create a crafting table by opening your inventory and put 4 wooden planks into your crafting box. Craft a wooden pick out of planks and sticks. Get a lot of cobblestone. You will need it to mine coal or mine some cobble to turn into a furnace, you then burn the logs in there for charcoal. Next, create a small house to wait out the night and make sure to lit it with torches. Then, carefully get out of your house and look out for monsters. If there are none, find more ores and wood to craft better tools and armor. Click here to find out more on crafting recipes.
