Mobs You Should Avoid

Creeper Enderman Zombie Spider Skeleton
Creepers are agressive creatures that will try to blow you into bits. You have to make sure to stay as far as possible from this walking tnt. Endermen are black, neutral mobs from the End that have the ability to teleport. Their abilities are the ability to pick up blocks and place them elsewhere and to teleport. When teleporting, an Enderman will leave a faint trail of purple particles leading to where it teleported to. Zombies are common hostile mobs that burn in sunlight. Spiders are common neutral mobs that have a unique ability to climb walls. At night, they are hostile, but at day they are neutral if you do not attack them. Skeletons are undead, ranged hostile mobs equipped with bows that burn in sunlight.
